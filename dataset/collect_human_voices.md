# Human Voice Collection Guide

- Record in a quiet environment with minimal background noise.
- Use a microphone positioned 15–30 cm from the mouth.
- Save audio as MP3 at 16–48 kHz, 15–60 seconds per clip.
- Speak naturally with varied prosody: reading, conversational, and emotional tones.
- Avoid sensitive content; agree to the consent statement below.
- File naming: `{language}_{speakerID}_{clipID}.mp3` (e.g., `tamil_S01_001.mp3`).
- Place files in `data/human/{language}/`.

## Quality Requirements
- Sample rate: 16–48 kHz
- Channels: mono or stereo
- Duration: 15–60 seconds
- No auto-tune or heavy post-processing
- Avoid clipping; keep levels in a comfortable range

## Consent Template
- I, [Speaker Name/ID], consent to the use of my recorded voice clips for research and evaluation of voice detection systems.
- I understand the clips may be used to train and validate models that differentiate human and AI-generated speech.
- I confirm I have the right to provide this audio and it does not include sensitive or private information.

Sign/Agree: ____________________   Date: _______________

## Suggested Script Instructions
- Read natural sentences suited to everyday conversation.
- Include pauses and emphasis changes.
- Vary speaking rate slightly between clips.
- Avoid background music or overlapping speakers.
